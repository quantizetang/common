code::XtremeApps: 2012

Date:

 27/04/2012
Time: 14:30-16:30
Venue:
School of Economics, NSR 5.2, Level 5
Singapore Management University
90 Stamford Road
Singapore 178903
Fee: Free

The code::Xtreme::Apps:: programming competition is back for the 6th straight year!

Over the years, code::XtremeApps:: Programming Competition has become ITSC's flagship competition and as always we aim to promote greater awareness of the use and value of programming standards and programming education.

This year's competition will be just as fun and exciting as before and it is suitable for people from all ages!

code::XtremeApps:: 2012 will continue to comprise two categories for entries --Junior (for 12 years and under) and Open (students and working professionals)

Join us at the code::XtremeApps:: Programming Competition launch to see what's in store for this year's competition and what fabulous prizes you can win!
